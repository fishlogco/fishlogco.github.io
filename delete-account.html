Delete Your Account

Permanently remove your account and all associated data

Important: This Action Is Permanent

Deleting your account will permanently remove all of your data from Fish Log. This action cannot be undone. Please make sure you want to proceed before following the steps below.

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your profile information (name, email, phone, photo)
  • All harvest reports you have submitted through the app
  • Catch photos stored in the app
  • Fishing statistics and achievements
  • Rewards program entries and history
  • Community catch feed posts
  • Feedback submissions

Note: Harvest reports that were already transmitted to the NC Division of Marine Fisheries cannot be deleted from their systems, as they are maintained by a government agency independent of Fish Log.

Option 1: Delete From the App

If you have access to the Fish Log app, this is the fastest way to delete your account:

1

Open Your Profile

Tap the menu icon, then tap "Profile" to go to your profile screen.

2

Enter Edit Mode

Tap the "Edit Profile" button at the top of your profile screen.

3

Scroll to Delete Account

Scroll to the bottom of the edit screen. You'll see a "Delete Account" section with a red button.

4

Confirm Deletion

Tap "Delete Account" and confirm twice when prompted. Your account and all data will be permanently deleted.

Option 2: Request Deletion by Email

If you cannot access the app or prefer to request deletion by email, send a message to the address below. Please include the email address associated with your Fish Log account so we can locate your data.

✉ Email Account Deletion Request

Send your request to: fishlogco@gmail.com
Subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
We will process your request within 7 business days and confirm by email once complete.

Non-Member / Anonymous Users

If you used Fish Log without creating a Rewards Member account, your harvest reports were submitted with a device identifier rather than a personal account. To request deletion of this data, email us at fishlogco@gmail.com and include any confirmation numbers from your submitted reports so we can locate your records.
