About Fish Log

Fish Log is a mobile app designed for North Carolina anglers. Starting December 2025, NC requires mandatory harvest reporting for five key species: Red Drum, Southern Flounder, Spotted Seatrout, Striped Bass, and Weakfish.

Fish Log makes this reporting simple, fast, and rewarding. Whether you're on the pier or in the boat, submit your reports in minutes and track your fishing journey over time.

NC Mandatory Harvest Reporting

North Carolina's mandatory harvest reporting program helps the Division of Marine Fisheries collect critical data for sustainable fisheries management. All anglers who catch and keep any of the five mandatory species must report their harvest. Fish Log is the easiest way to stay compliant.
